About

Story Behind the Project

From a personal experience of our entrepreneur Croc, where she was planning a customised trip for a friend. Due to the rareness of requested trip content, there exists a huge price difference between a ready-made tour a self-planed due to low demand, leading us to the idea of creating an app where users can upload or listen to the attractions of different interests with low, or even no cost.

CurrentBusiness Model

How could anyone benefit? Listeners can choose to reward the uploader if they had pleasant experience with the audio guide, thus uploaders get paid and motivated, while listners enjoyed their trip with additional support. A win-win situation for them both.

Why we chose Audio Guide

The reasons we chose to present guide in audio form are, firstly, travellers can pace themselves and tour with minimal distraction. Secondly, it provides the option to travel with extra comfort as some might feel unease with the presence of a stranger(guide). Thirdly, guides are no longer limited by their location. They can now do "cross country business" whenever and wherever they want to.
